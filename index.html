Sweet Potatoes
1. Wash the sweet potatoes. You can leave the skin on!
2. Preheat oven to 375º.
3. Cut sweet potatoes in half.
4. Use a fork to poke a few holes in each piece.
5. Place the sweet potatoes on a baking tray.
6. Drizzle olive oil on the sweet potatoes.
7. Bake for 45 minutes!
Broccoli
1. Wash broccoli.
2. Preheat oven to 450º.
3. Cut broccoli into bite-size pieces.
4. Place broccoli on baking tray.
5. Drizzle olive oil on the pieces.
6. Season with your own spices - even just salt and pepper works great!
7. Bake for 20 minutes!
Salmon
1. Place salmon on aluminum foil.
2. Preheat oven to 375º.
3. Drizzle olive oil on salmon.
4. Season salmon to your preferences - you can use spices, lemon, soy sauce, etc.
5. Wrap salmon completely in aluminum foil.
6. Bake for 30 minutes!
